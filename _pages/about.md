I am currently a PhD candidate in Physics at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. 

Prior to this, I earned my Bachelor’s degree from the School of Physics at [Sun Yat-sen University](https://www.sysu.edu.cn/sysuen/) under the supervision of [Prof. Ren Jie](https://inspirehep.net/authors/1029004), where my undergraduate research focused on the AdS/CFT correspondence, QNMs of black holes, and Virasoro conformal blocks.
